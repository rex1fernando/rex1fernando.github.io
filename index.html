Rex Fernando

rex@cs.ucla.edu · cv

I am a PhD student at UCLA studying under Amit Sahai. I am interested in Cryptography. Before coming to UCLA, I did a Masters at UW - Madison. While there I did research with Eric Bach.

I spent the summer of 2019 working with Alon Rosen at the FACT Center in IDC Herzliya. During the first half of 2020, I was an intern at NTT Research in the Bay Area, working with Ilan Komargodski.
